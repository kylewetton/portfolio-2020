Dynamic Colour Changing

Colour changing bitmapped images

An idea I had was a novel way of tackling dynamically colour adjusting bitmap images, an SVG is overlayed on top of a PNG, and using the CSS filter attribute I multiplied the colour onto the PNG. I posted this on Twitter and was pleased to see it go viral. It placed within the top 10 Codepens for 2019 and was shared with praise by Addy Osami, the Senior Engineering Manager at Google and an article was written about it by Chris Coyer, the founder of CodePen and CSS Tricks. I later wrote a tutorial at Codrops to document the novel idea.
